Superheroine Justice League

Trading cards of female leaders & their superheroine alter egos!
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

An application developed by Team Spitfire Athlete during #ChimeHack to inspire women around the world and make women leaders more visible.

What if inspiring female leaders had their own trading cards? What if you could learn about their impact, interesting facts, and how they went after their goals?

In the Superheroine Justice League app, we take a fun twist and also share these women's powerful superheroine alter egos.

In this app, you can make trading cards of women who inspire you, share those cards on Twitter, and spread the word about the changes they are fighting for.

This app is about women bringing to light the accomplishments of other women. And why superheroines? Because deep down inside, we are all superheroines waiting to be unleashed.
